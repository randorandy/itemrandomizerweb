Change Log:

Rusty - 2/17/2022

  • There will be casual and Tournament logic. All of these should be rotated with strotlog's tool:
    • https://strotlog.github.io/randoxrotation/
    • Casual logic can only require easy hellruns, the top of suitless maridia with high jump, and suits will not be in acid.
    • Tournament logic can require most tricks that are possible in rotation, including underwater bomb jumping and underwater wall jumping, and lava/acid baths. No OOB, xray climbs, or door clips are required.
